Charter of Affection

Being a True & Solemn Declaration

Know all Men by these Presents, that I, Anthony, being of sound Mind and devoted Heart, do hereby set forth this Charter of Affection in Honor of one Miss Aspen, she being most worthy of all Esteem and Tender Regard.

Wherefore It Is Proclaimed

That on this Day when the World doth celebrate Girlfriends — Thou hast always been more than a mere Title. Thou hast been a Muse, a Memory, a Mirror most true. Thou hast transformed Distance into something Sacred, Something beautiful to long for in the quiet Hours.
❦
And even when Silence did stand between us, My Heart never ceased speaking thy dear Name.
❦
Thou art loved this Day — Fiercely, quietly, and most completely.
Happy Girlfriend Day

In Witness Whereof, I have hereunto set my Hand and Seal

This Day of Our Lord
Two Thousand and Twenty-Five
Anthony
Devoted Servant
who loves thee more than himself
